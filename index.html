About Us


Our startup is an ambitious electric vehicle manufacturer working towards bringing fast & efficient transition from gasoline vehicles to clean energy vehicles, which will be within the reach of masses. With utmost priority given to safety, sustainability & efficiency, we are pushing ourselves towards building a better & greener future for coming generations.


Our Mission


Even a small step into the right direction proves to be significant in the future. We at Wattt Electromotive believe that its our responsibility to save the Earth from disruption caused by the emission of green house gasses by providing safe, sustainable and efficient alternatives for transportation to the people.
